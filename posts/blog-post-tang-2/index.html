Grammar : Might and May

PRESENT PAST
SUBJECT + MIGHT + VERB SUBJECT + MIGHT HAVE + PP
She might be English She might have gone to England
She might speak English She might have learnt English at school
She might be speaking She might have been speaking to her friend
N.B. MIGHT = MAY N.B. Negation is ‘not’
