Grammar : Might and May

PRESENT PAST
SUBJECT + MIGHT + VERB SUBJECT + MIGHT + HAVE + PP
She might be English She might have spoken to him
She might speak English She might have gone to England
She might be speaking She might have been playing the piano
N.B. MIGHT = MAY
